## ðŸš€ Core Features & Usage Guide

### 1. Real-Time Risk Detection
**Target User:** Compliance Analyst
*   **Function:** Gemini AI analyzes every transaction as it enters the network.
*   **How to Use:**
    1.  Navigate to the `Dashboard`.
    2.  Watch the "Live Transactions" feed.
    3.  High-risk transactions turn **RED** automatically.
    4.  Click on any Alert to see the Gemini explanation ("Why was this marked?").

### 2. One-Click AI SAR Filing
**Target User:** Compliance Officer
*   **Function:** Automatically drafts Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) for FinCEN.
*   **How to Use:**
    1.  Go to the `Alerts` page.
    2.  Select a "Critical" alert.
    3.  Click "File SAR".
    4.  In the form, click **"âœ¨ Auto-Fill with Gemini"**.
    5.  Watch as the narrative, suspicion type, and dates are pre-filled instantly.
    6.  Review and Submit.

### 3. Immutable Audit Trail
**Target User:** External Regulator / Auditor
*   **Function:** Every compliance action is cryptographically signed and logged.
*   **How to Verify:**
    1.  Navigate to the `Audit Log` page.
    2.  Copy the "Transaction Hash" of any action.
    3.  Verify it on the Arc Network Block Explorer.
    4.  Proof that "Officer Alice approved this transaction at 10:42 AM" is mathematical.

### 4. Signed PDF Exports
*   **Function:** Generate monthly reports that are digitally signed.
*   **Feature:** Each PDF contains a QR code linked to the blockchain proof.
